Cut Out Sugar Cookies

Description

This recipe is BY FAR my favorite sugar cookie recipe EVER. Each batch turns out perfectly every time and are perfect for decorating. We love making these for just about any occasion and they are so easy, they kids can help make them too! Keep reading to learn all of our tips, tricks, and secrets to making these cookies and our favorite ways to decorate them.

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup sugar
  • ¼ cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter softened
  • 1/4 cup shortening
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
  • ¼ teaspoon almond extract
  • 3 cups flour
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup flour (for rolling out)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400-degrees F.
  2. In the bowl of a stand mixer, combine sugar, powdered sugar, butter, and shortening. Mix until well combined.
  3. Add eggs, vanilla, and almond extract and mix until combined. Scrape the sides of the bowl.
  4. In a medium-sized bowl whisk together the dry ingredients - flour, salt, and baking powder.
  5. Add flour mixture to sugar mixture a little at a time. Mix until the dough pulls away from the sides and forms a soft ball when pressed together.
  6. Remove dough from bowl and roll out onto a lightly floured surface (about ¼ cup flour). Roll out to 3/8-inch thickness
  7. Cut out shapes using cookie cutters.
  8. Bake cookies on a lined baking sheet for 7 minutes (6 minutes for smaller cookies). Remove cookies from pan and allow to cool on a cooling rack.
  9. After cookies have completely cooled, they are ready for decorating.
