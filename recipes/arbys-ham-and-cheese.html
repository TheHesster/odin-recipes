Arby's Ham and Cheese

Description

You can't go wrong with an Arby's Ham and Cheese! It is served warm with toasted bread. The cheese is melted perfectly and drizzled over the top of the sliced ham. Altogether the combination is out of this world amazing and will keep you from going back to Arby's again.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound deli ham thinly sliced
  • 1 cup Arby's sauce to taste (see below)
  • 8 onion buns
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • ¾ cup milk or more if needed
  • 1 to 2 cups cheddar cheese shredded by hand, not pre-packaged
  • salt & pepper to taste
  • ½ cup ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • Frank's Red Hot Sauce to taste (optional)

Steps

  1. In a medium-sized skillet over medium heat, cook deli ham until heated through (spray the pan with cooking spray to reduce sticking).
  2. Spread Arby's sauce on one side of each bun and place ham on the bun.
  3. Spoon on cheddar cheese sauce (see below) and serve.

    4. For the cheese sauce

  4. Melt butter in a small saucepan over medium heat. Whisk in the flour (whisking constantly) and cook for about 1 minute forming a roux.
  5. Slowly whisk in milk until it reaches the desired consistency.
  6. Remove from heat and stir in cheese. Add salt and pepper, to taste.

    7. For the Arby's sauce

  7. In a small saucepan combine all the Arby's sauce ingredients.
  8. Simmer over medium heat for a couple of minutes.
